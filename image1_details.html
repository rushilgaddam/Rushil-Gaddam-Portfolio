Project Explanation

In this project, lattice grids were created to map the locations and interactions of different cell types within tissue samples from 4 cancer patients. By overlaying a grid onto spatial data, we can examine how cells are organized and how they interact with each other within defined areas. This spatial organization is crucial for understanding how different cell types contribute to tumor growth, immune response, and the efficacy of treatments. There were many cell types used and they were compared among deep amd superfical tissues
